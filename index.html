About Fadan Consultant Limited

Established in 2015, Fadan Consultant Limited is a premier consulting firm based in Nairobi and a proud member of the Strathwell Group. We specialize in providing strategic solutions for the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) sector across East Africa.

Our team of experienced consultants works closely with manufacturers, distributors, and retailers to optimize distribution networks, develop effective route-to-market strategies, and enhance overall market performance.

With a deep understanding of local markets and consumer behavior, we help our clients navigate the complexities of the FMCG landscape, driving sustainable growth and competitive advantage.
